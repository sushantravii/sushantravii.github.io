About

I direct.
I obsess.
I deliver.

Based somewhere between a viewfinder and a deadline — I'm Sushant Ravitchandirane, a creative director and filmmaker who treats every project as a singular act of intention. From concept to color grade, I control the narrative.

My work spans documentary, branded content, music visuals, and independent film. I believe in restraint, silence, and the power of a single well-placed cut.
